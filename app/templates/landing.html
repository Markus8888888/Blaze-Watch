Predict Wildfires with AI.

Blaze Watch is an early warning system for wildfire movement. It uses advanced AI and real-time satellite data to track fire behaviour in Canada. Whether you are an emergency responder or a local resident, Blaze Watch provides the insight you need before the danger increases.
